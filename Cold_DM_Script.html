The 3 Golden Rules of Cold DMs
01.
Never pitch in message 1. The first message earns the right to send a second message. That's it. Research them, mention something specific, ask one question.
02.
Personalise or don't send. If you can send the same DM to 100 people without changing a word, it's not a DM — it's spam. One specific detail about their business changes everything.
03.
The demo closes more than any pitch. Offer to build something for free before asking for money. A working demo is worth more than any portfolio link or testimonial.
the 5 scripts
Copy. Personalise. Send.
Script 01
The Permission Opener
WhatsApp · Instagram · LinkedIn — use this first, always
When to use: First message to any cold prospect
Hi [Name], I noticed [Business Name] on [where you found them]. I work with local businesses in [City] to automate their customer responses and lead generation using AI.

Would it be okay if I sent you a quick voice note explaining how it works for businesses like yours?
→ Always ask permission before pitching. Response rate doubles. The voice note offer feels personal, not corporate.
Script 02
The Social Proof Follow-Up
Send this after they say yes to the first message
When to use: After they reply or say "sure, tell me more"
Thanks [Name]! So I built a small AI assistant for a [similar business type] here in [City]. It answers customer questions on WhatsApp automatically — 24 hours a day, even at 2am.

They saved about 2 hours a day on customer service alone. I can build something similar for [Business Name] in 3–4 days.

Want me to show you how it would look specifically for your business?
→ Make the result specific and local. "A business in Karachi" lands harder than "a business I worked with."
Script 03
The Free Demo Offer
This one closes more clients than any paid ad
When to use: After they show interest in the concept
I'll build you a quick free demo — no charge, no commitment, no pressure.

Just send me your most common customer questions, your menu or services list, and your opening hours. I'll have a working demo ready for you within 24 hours.

You test it. If it saves you time, we talk. If not, you've lost nothing.
→ "You've lost nothing" removes all risk objections. This single line gets more yeses than anything else.
Script 04
The Price Close
After the demo is shared and they're interested
When to use: After they've seen and tested your demo
Here's your demo → [link]. This is exactly what your customers would experience on WhatsApp.

To deploy this live on your WhatsApp number, I charge PKR [8,000–15,000] one-time setup + PKR 3,000/month to keep it updated as your business changes.

Want to move forward this week?
→ Always price in PKR for local clients. Start lower than you think, raise after your first 3 testimonials. Don't negotiate on the monthly retainer.
Script 05
The 48-Hour Follow-Up
For when they go quiet after showing interest
When to use: 48 hours after any message with no reply
Hey [Name], just checking in on the demo I sent. I'm taking on 2 more businesses in [City] this month — wanted to make sure you had first right of refusal since you were interested.

Let me know either way — no pressure at all!
→ Scarcity + no pressure. This follow-up alone recovers 30% of dead leads. "Let me know either way" gives them permission to say no, which paradoxically makes them more likely to say yes.
