Schedule LinkedIn posts a week in advanceBuffer Free
Write all 7 posts on Sunday. Buffer auto-posts them at peak times daily. Never miss a day, never last-minute panic.

Turn YouTube videos into LinkedIn posts automaticallyn8n + ChatGPT
Paste a YouTube URL → n8n extracts the transcript → ChatGPT rewrites it as a carousel hook, caption, and tweet thread. Done in 30 seconds.

Auto-generate 3 content angles from trending postsn8n + RSS + ChatGPT
n8n monitors RSS feeds in your niche. When something goes viral, ChatGPT spits out 3 LinkedIn angles for you. Fresh ideas delivered daily without scrolling.

5 tasks
Build a local business lead list from Google Mapsn8n + Sheets
