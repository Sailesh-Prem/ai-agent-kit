client pitch script
The 5-Step DM That Books Clients

Use this script to approach local business owners on WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, or LinkedIn. Works for restaurants, clinics, salons, coaching businesses, and e-commerce stores in Pakistan.

Message 1 — The opener (day 1)
Hi [Name], I noticed [Business Name] on [Instagram/Google Maps/LinkedIn]. I work with local businesses in [City] to automate their customer responses and lead generation using AI. Would it be okay if I sent you a quick 60-second voice note explaining how it works?
→ Always ask permission first. Response rate doubles when you ask before pitching.
Message 2 — The proof (after they say yes)
Thanks! So basically I built a small AI assistant for a [similar business] here in [City]. It answers customer questions on WhatsApp automatically — 24/7, even at 2am. They saved about 2 hours a day on customer service. I can build something similar for you in 3–4 days. Want me to show you how it would look for [Business Name] specifically?
→ Make it specific to their business. Generic pitches get ignored.
Message 3 — The free demo offer (if they're interested)
I'll build you a quick free demo — no charge, no commitment. Just send me your most common customer questions, your menu / services, and your opening hours. I'll have a working demo ready for you within 24 hours.
→ A free demo closes more clients than any pitch ever will.
Message 4 — The close (after demo is shared)
Here's your demo → [link]. This is exactly what your customers would experience. To deploy this live on your WhatsApp number, I charge PKR [8,000–15,000] one-time setup + PKR 3,000/month to keep it updated. Want to move forward?
→ Price in PKR. Local pricing = local trust. Start low, raise as you grow.
Message 5 — The follow-up (if no reply in 48 hrs)
Hey [Name], just checking in on the demo I sent. I'm taking on 2 more businesses in [City] this month — wanted to make sure you had first right of refusal since you were interested. Let me know either way!
→ Scarcity + no pressure. This follow-up alone recovers 30% of dead leads.
client explainer
What Is an AI Agent?
Tell your clients in plain Urdu-English
the simple explanation
Think of it as a digital employee that never sleeps, never asks for a raise, and never makes a mistake twice.

An AI agent is software that can take instructions, think through a problem, use tools (like browsing the web, reading emails, sending messages), and complete tasks — automatically, 24/7. You give it a goal. It figures out the steps. You review the result.

what it can do for local businesses

Answer customer questions on WhatsApp at 3am when you're asleep

Find new leads by searching the web and sending outreach messages automatically

Write your social media posts every week based on your business updates

Send follow-up messages to every person who enquired but never bought
